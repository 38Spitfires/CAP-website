About Me

Hi there! I am 38Spitfires. I code on Scratch, CodeTorch, Code.org, and now GitHub! I am learning HTML (kind of - this website was made on an HTML template). I am fascinated by aircraft design, aviation history, and natural disaster science. I am the creator of the pyro-tornado database website. My username comes from my favorite aircraft (the Supermarine Spitfire, a British WW2 fighter) and my favorite musical (Come From Away, about the 38 planes that landed in Gander, Newfoundland when the US airspace closed after 9/11). My favorite aircraft are the Spitfire, Beech Staggerwing (1930s sport biplane), and Douglas DC-3 (1930s airliner). The natural disasters I find most interesting are volcanic eruptions, hurricanes, tornadoes, and wildfires. My favorite volcano is Eyjafjallajökull in Iceland. I grew up watching PBS NOVA, so now you are required to watch Deadliest Volcanoes, the documentary that inspired me to pursue Earth Sciences. Yes, Eyjafjallajökull is featured in it.
